About Our Website

Created by 'Virtual Environmentalists', this website aims to engage students in learning about the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal number 14 - protection of life below the water. Students can play educational games and learn about the destruction of natural marine habitats and ecosystems.

It is so important to engage young people in protecting our oceans, as oceans cover three quarters of the Earth’s surface, contain 97% of the Earth’s water, and represent 99% of the living space on the planet by volume.
